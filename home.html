Gandhi Institute of Technology and Management Hyderabad Campus (GITAM Hyderabad Campus) is an institute of higher education located in Hyderabad, Telangana, India. It is one of 3 campuses of Gandhi Institute of Technology and Management. The campus admitted its first batch of students in 2009.

Vishakapatnam Campus
Established in 1980 as a main-campus of GITAM. Nestled in between the valley of Eastern Ghats and Bay of Bengal, the location is the most idyllic places in Andhra Pradesh.

Hyderabad Campus
Established in 2009 as an off-campus of GITAM. Since then the campus has grown into a well acclaimed hub of higher education in the state of Telangana.

Bangalore Campus
Established in 2012, with modern infrastructure supported by dedicated faculty and administrative staff. The campus is located in an ideal environment in Karnataka.
